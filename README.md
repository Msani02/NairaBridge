# 🌉 NairaBridge — Cross-Border E-commerce & Logistics Infrastructure (Nigeria ↔ China)

[![Project Status: Active](https://img.shields.io/badge/Project%20Status-Active-emerald.svg)]()
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-teal.svg)](LICENSE)

**NairaBridge** is a cross-border e-commerce and logistics platform that enables Nigerians to purchase goods from verified Chinese merchants, pay in Naira, and receive their products through a coordinated shipping and warehouse system managed by on-ground agents in China.

The platform simplifies international sourcing by combining **local payment infrastructure, escrow protection, and logistics coordination** into a single seamless system.

---

## 🚩 Problem Statement

Cross-border trade between Nigeria and China is hindered by:

- **Payment Barriers:** Difficulty making secure international payments  
- **Logistics Inefficiencies:** Fragmented shipping processes and unpredictable delivery timelines  
- **Trust Deficit:** Risk of fraud when dealing with unverified suppliers  
- **Lack of Physical Presence:** No reliable way to verify goods before shipment  

---

## 💡 Solution

NairaBridge provides an integrated system that solves these challenges through:

- **Local Currency Payments (₦):** Users pay in Naira via a structured payment system (integration-ready for Interswitch)  
- **Escrow Protection:** Funds are securely held until order processing milestones are met  
- **Agent-Based Operations:** Verified agents in China manage merchant onboarding, quality checks, and logistics  
- **Warehouse & Container Optimization:** Orders are grouped into shipping containers to reduce cost and improve efficiency  
- **End-to-End Tracking:** Transparent order lifecycle from purchase to delivery  

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Browse Products:** Users explore products uploaded by verified merchants  
2. **Place Order:** Users select items and proceed to checkout  
3. **Make Payment:** Payment is made in Naira and held in escrow  
4. **Agent Processing:** Agent confirms order and receives goods at the warehouse  
5. **Order Consolidation:** Items are grouped into shipping containers  
6. **Shipment:** Container is dispatched when threshold is reached  
7. **Delivery:** Items are delivered to users in Nigeria  

---

## 👥 User Roles

### 👤 User (Buyer)
- Browse and search products  
- Place orders and track delivery  
- Manage wallet and transactions  

### 🧑‍🔧 Agent (China-Based)
- Create and manage merchant accounts  
- Upload and manage products  
- Coordinate warehouse and shipping  
- Update order status  

### 🛠 Admin
- Monitor platform activity  
- Manage users, agents, and transactions  
- Handle disputes and escrow release
